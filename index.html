Wikitweets

Wikitweets is a collection of Twitter bots about Wikipedia, Wikimedia Commons and other wikis:
  • @commonspotd: Picture of the day in Wikimedia Commons
  • @enwikipotd: Picture of the day in English Wikipedia
  • @enwikinewpages: New pages in English Wikipedia
  • @wikiexcerpts
Look at the source code. Please, report bugs and send suggestions to my talk page.
